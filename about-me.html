Hi, I'm Josephina, a design student at USC Roski, minoring in Designing Products under IYA, with a foundation in fine arts. My practice blends visual communication, hands-on prototyping, and material exploration to develop concept-driven products and experiences. Grounded in close observation and cultural context, I'm drawn to creating lifestyle-focused design that thoughtfully balances function, form, and craft.

Outside of design, you can find me painting, baking, and spending time outdoors, whether that's hiking, camping, or just being near the water. I also play piano, which I've always enjoyed as a way to unwind and keep my hands busy. I'm someone who loves trying new foods and drinks and learning about different cultures, especially through local cuisine when I travel. At home, that often turns into experimenting in the kitchen and sourcing ingredients locally. These are the things that help me recharge, stay curious, and feel balanced outside of school and projects.
