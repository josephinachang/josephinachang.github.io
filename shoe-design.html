HIKING
SHOES

This medium-fidelity footwear design project focuses on the development of hiking shoes through a combination of market research, concept ideation, and hands-on prototyping. Initial competitor and market analysis was conducted collaboratively to identify strengths, gaps, and opportunities within the hiking footwear space. Building on these insights, I independently developed and refined my own design through structured sketching and Illustrator renderings, then fabricated a physical prototype using vacuum-sealed base shoe models and custom sole construction. The final outcome balances functional performance, material exploration, and visual identity specific to hiking use.
