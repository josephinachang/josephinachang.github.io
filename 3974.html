3974 is a conceptual coffee kiosk branding project designed for USC's Roski Courtyard. The name references the construction years of the Roski buildings that the courtyard is nestled between: Watt Hall (1939) and Harris Hall (1974), grounding the identity in the site's history. Responding to student feedback that the courtyard feels cold and uninviting, the branding introduces a bright, arts-forward visual language to soften the brutalist environment. The kiosk is designed to nest naturally within the courtyard, enhancing the space without disrupting existing circulation or student activity while bringing warmth and energy to the environment.
