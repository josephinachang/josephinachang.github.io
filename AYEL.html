"ARE YOU EVEN LISTENING?" is a conceptual fundraising concert and branding project benefiting Habitat for Humanity, supporting the rebuilding of homes lost in the Eaton Fire. Designed for 500 students in USC's Watt Hall Courtyard, the event features a Pop-Punk/Emo Revival lineup with WILLOW, Meet Me @ The Altar, and KennyHoopla. I developed the event's visual identity, promotional materials, and production strategy, working within a conceptual $25,000 Whole Foods sponsorship as a real-world design constraint to explore how branding and experiential design can drive engagement and social impact.
