PARCH is a sauce-inspired typeface developed through a typographic exploration of texture, modularity, and cultural reference. Influenced by the organic unpredictability of squirting out hot sauce from the bottle, each letterform is built on a bead-like grid that mimics the irregular flow of sauce while maintaining a cohesive, semi-modular structure. The result is a tight, condensed typeface with a playful, expressive character that supports dynamic composition.

Rooted in my personal relationship with spicy food and its significance within Asian cuisine, the typeface extends beyond form into application. Bold, punchy color palettes and food-based contexts highlight PARCH's versatility across branding and packaging, celebrating spice as both a visual and cultural experience.
