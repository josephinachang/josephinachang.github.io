Meta Ranger is a 3D material design project exploring toy packaging design through customization and identity. Drawing from my childhood love for Power Rangers and the lack of representation in its lead characters, I designed myself as a "star ranger" action figure. The prototype was sculpted in clay and paired with commercially inspired packaging using fabrication methods such as vacuum sealing to emulate retail toy production. While the figure functions as a self-portrait, the broader concept explores a marketable, customizable product. Through personalized features like face, color, and identity, kids get to see themselves as the hero, transforming imaginative play into a more inclusive and empowering experience.
