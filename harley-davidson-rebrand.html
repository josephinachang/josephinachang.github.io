This conceptual brand transformation reimagines Harley-Davidson as an urban mobility brand, moving the focus from patriotic, open-highway freedom to everyday city movement through e-scooters and e-bikes. Guided by the Stanford d.School Design Sprint framework, the project began with empathy-based research. It evolved into a new visual and narrative direction, reshaping the brand's tone, values, and messaging for a younger, urban audience while preserving its underlying sense of independence.
